# PourStock — Executive Summary

**AI-Powered Operations Platform for Hotels & Restaurants**
*Investor-Ready Summary — March 2026*

---

## The Problem

Hospitality is one of the last industries still running on fragmented, manual operations tooling. A typical 50-room hotel with a restaurant manages check-in on a PMS, dinner reservations on printed PDFs, beverage stock on spreadsheets, and housekeeping on whiteboards. There is no real-time coordination layer between departments. This costs hotels money through waste, misallocated labour, and service failures — particularly during live dinner service, where timing errors translate directly into guest dissatisfaction.

Existing software either solves one narrow problem (point-of-sale, PMS, reservations) or is enterprise-grade and priced out of reach for independent and mid-market hotels. The mid-market — thousands of hotels across the Nordics and Europe — is critically underserved.

---

## The Solution

PourStock is a multi-tenant SaaS platform that unifies hotel and restaurant operations into a single real-time system. It replaces the entire fragmented stack with one purpose-built tool designed for tablets and phones during live service.

**Core capabilities:**
- **AI-Powered Table Planning** — Upload a reservation PDF; AI extracts all guest data, auto-assigns tables, and generates a live-service floor plan with arrival timers and preparation summaries.
- **Beverage Inventory** — Full product catalog, multi-location stock tracking, fast mobile count workflows, and automatic reorder suggestions.
- **Hotel Reception & Housekeeping** — Real-time room board, check-in/out flows, and event-driven housekeeping task generation.
- **Purchasing & Ordering** — Integrated vendor management and purchase order workflows.
- **Operational Reports** — Occupancy analytics, RevPAR tracking, beverage cost-of-goods, and variance reporting.
- **Real-Time Multi-Device Sync** — All views synchronize instantly across all devices on the hotel network.

---

## Traction

PourStock is **live in production** at Sønderborg Strand Hotel, Denmark. The platform has completed a 12-phase architectural migration program, establishing a normalized, multi-tenant data foundation capable of supporting multiple hotel properties. The system handles daily dinner service coordination, continuous inventory management, and front-office operations.

Key technical milestones achieved:
- Full multi-tenant isolation via Row Level Security
- AI reservation parsing with configurable per-hotel parser profiles and SHA-256 content caching
- Structured billing model (folios, payments) ready for commercial activation
- Soak validation framework accumulating parity evidence for domain-by-domain system cutovers
